🏥 Maternal Health+

📰 The challenge

Maternal mortality remains critically high; many deaths are preventable with basic equipment, protocols, and referral. Rural clinics lack minimal obstetric kits and reliable cold chains for vaccines.

  • Low share of births attended by skilled staff in remote areas
  • Weak cold chain in rural health posts
  • Need for training, supervision, and monthly indicators
✅ Our approach

A systemic model: equip, train, protocolise. No equipment roll-out without training; no training without follow-up.

  • Pillar 1 — Clinic equipment: standard obstetric kits, solar fridges, oxygen where needed
  • Pillar 2 — Midwife training: emergencies (PPH, pre-eclampsia), monthly cascade sessions
  • Pillar 3 — Protocols & referral: illustrated Malagasy guides, SMS referral to district hospitals, public dashboard
