⚡ Community Solar Energy

📰 The challenge

Electrification rates remain low; isolated rural areas depend on expensive, polluting diesel generators. Reliable clean power unlocks clinics, schools, and small enterprises.

  • High share of energy cost in rural SME budgets
  • Broken cold chains for vaccines without stable electricity
  • Climate exposure and need for long-lived infrastructure
✅ Our approach

Each mini-grid is sized for a whole village cluster — homes, school, clinic, small shops — with local maintenance jobs.

  • Pillar 1 — Mini-grid hardware: solar + batteries + low-voltage distribution + prepaid metering
  • Pillar 2 — Local jobs: trained village energy technicians employed by community committees
  • Pillar 3 — Governance & finance: mobile payments, renewal fund, annual emissions audit
